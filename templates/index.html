About

Club Management System

Club management system is an all-in-one solution that helps clubs grow their membership while saving time and efforts. It does this by automating administrative tasks associated with running a member-based organization, as well as offering tools and features that elevate their member experience.
Club Management System helps users manage dance studios, sports teams, yoga studios, martial art clubs and fitness. It tracks members. Key features include account relationship management, custom data fields, risk reporting, gallery management.

Our Services

Managing Clubs

Here we basically manage all sorts of clubs, Dance Club, Technical Club, Sports Club, etc.

Club Members

Use Club Management System as an inexpensive way to automate nearly all of their administrative work so they can get back to what's important: enjoying their club's activities and creating value for their members.

Managing Events

The clubs primary focus is to work in collaboration with the Student council members-organising and managing all events hosted by them from intra-collegiate events such as the Fresher's Talents/National fest in the college.
